FAQ

Common
questions

Can't find your answer? Contact us →

What exactly is included in the $50/month Solo plan?
The Solo plan gives you the full Synapse IPS engine on one machine — neural anomaly detection, deep packet inspection, automatic IP quarantine, the live dashboard, and support for both macOS and Windows. Everything you need to protect a single device with enterprise-grade AI.
Is there a free trial before I commit?
Yes — all plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. You get full access to every feature in your chosen plan from day one.
How does the annual billing discount work?
When you choose annual billing, you pay for 10 months and get 2 months free — a 20% saving. The Solo plan drops from $50/month to $40/month, and the Team plan from $120/month to $96/month.
Does Synapse IPS require any special hardware?
No. Synapse IPS is a software-only solution. It runs directly on your machine using the existing network interface — no dedicated hardware or external appliances required.
What makes AI detection better than signature-based tools?
Signature-based tools only detect known threats. Synapse IPS learns normal behaviour on your network and flags deviations — catching zero-day threats, novel attack patterns, and suspicious behaviour no signature has ever seen.
Can I cancel or change my plan at any time?
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your account dashboard. Monthly plans cancel at the end of the billing period with no penalties.
